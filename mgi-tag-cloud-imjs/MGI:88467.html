    Mutations in this locus cause variable phenotype, from embryonic lethal to viable/fertile with altered fibrillogenesis. Homozygotes can show impaired bone formation and fragility, osteoporosis, dermal fibrosis, impaired uterine postpartum involution, and aortic dissection.
